PokeVR

WebXR Pokemon Catching Experience

HOW TO PLAY

  • In VR (SteamVR): Swing your right controller and release the Trigger to throw Pokéballs.
  • On Desktop (Non-VR testing): Left-click anywhere on the screen to throw a Pokéball forward.
  • Objective: Hit the yellow 3D Pokémon to catch it. Successful catch is randomized (like the games!).
